Organic Gardening Techniques

Organic gardening focuses on using natural methods to cultivate plants. This guide covers key techniques:

  • Soil Health: Enhance soil fertility naturally with compost, manure, or cover crops.
  • Companion Planting: Grow plants together that benefit each other to naturally repel pests or improve growth.
  • Pest Management: Employ integrated pest management with natural predators and barriers.
  • Water Conservation: Use efficient watering systems like drip irrigation to save water.
  • Seed Saving: Preserve biodiversity by saving seeds from heirloom varieties.
  • Weed Control: Manage weeds manually or with organic mulches, avoiding synthetic herbicides.
